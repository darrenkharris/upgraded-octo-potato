Questions people ask

Straight answers before you get in touch.

Do you support both home users and organisations?

Yes. The support model is tailored to the situation. Home users usually need patient troubleshooting and setup help. Organisations usually need a wider view of risk, resilience, cost and support arrangements.

Can you help with Cyber Essentials?

Yes. We can help you understand the controls, identify gaps and prepare for certification. Certification itself should be completed through an approved Cyber Essentials certification body.

Can you work with schools and charities on limited budgets?

Yes. Recommendations are prioritised by risk, impact and affordability so you know what to do first and what can wait.

Do you replace our existing IT provider?

Not always. We can provide independent review, project support, second opinion work, strategic planning or specialist help alongside existing support arrangements.
